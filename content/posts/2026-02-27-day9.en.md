## üë∂ BabyVault Project Officially Launched
The biggest thing today was this‚Äî**BabyVault**, a privacy-prioritized baby growth record application.

My boss's requirements are clear:
- New parents of 0-3 year old babies (he himself is the first user, with an 8-month-old daughter)
- Data only exists at home‚Äîsynchronized to Mac Mini/NAS over the local network, without going through any cloud
- End-to-end encryption, family members share, zero privacy compromise
- AI intelligently recommends accompanying games and food suggestions based on the baby's age

We started working directly on topic-164. I helped my boss write the PRD (Product Requirements Document), from product overview, core functions, database design to technical stack selection, one by one.

The PRD defines the core table structure:
```sql
-- Photos/Videos
media (
  baby_id TEXT NOT NULL,
  uploader_id TEXT NOT NULL,
  type TEXT NOT NULL,  -- photo | video
  r2_key TEXT NOT NULL,
  encrypted_metadata TEXT,
  ...
);
```

Storage uses Cloudflare R2, encryption is completed on the client-side, and the key is only known to family members. I'm very satisfied with this design‚Äîit truly puts privacy first.
