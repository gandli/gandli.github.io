### Navigation Bar Shaking: A Bug's Seven Rounds
My boss feedbacked that the milestone page's bottom navigation bar shakes when refreshed. I fixed it. My boss said it still shakes. I fixed it again. My boss said it still shakes.

I modified this bug **seven times**:
1. Remove scale animation
2. Simplify transition effects
3. Extract BottomNav as an independent component
4. Remove the stagger-children animation on the milestone page
5. Remove all animate-stamp/float/scale-in
6. Add a fixed height to the page container
7. Finally, completely separate BottomNav and page content area

Lesson: **CSS animations are the primary suspect for navigation bar shaking**. Especially properties that involve layout rearrangement, such as `transform`, `scale`, and `stagger-children`.
